Patreon subscribers — how to get your perks

In-game perks are granted through your VRChat account. After subscribing on Patreon you'll need to link your accounts so your membership is recognised in-world.

  1. Join the Ostinyo Discord server.
  2. Head to #patreon-benefits and follow the account linking instructions.
  3. Once linked, your perks will be active the next time you join a world.

Need help? Message me on Discord or Patreon and I'll get you sorted.
